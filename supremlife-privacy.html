Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: SupremLife does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. The SupremLife application uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish luxury lifestyle videos. We do not access data from TikTok users beyond what is strictly necessary to post to authorized accounts.

This Privacy Policy describes how SupremLife ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our content platform and TikTok API integration.

1. Who We Are

SupremLife is a luxury lifestyle content publishing platform. The SupremLife app creates and publishes daily video content to TikTok, covering supercars, luxury timepieces, private aviation, and high-end travel — targeting audiences aged 18–30.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

SupremLife is a content publishing platform, not a data collection service. We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information from viewers.
  • Store payment or financial information of any kind.
  • Access, collect, or store data from other TikTok users' accounts or profiles.
  • Track individual users across websites or platforms.
  • Build audience profiles or sell data to third parties.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

The SupremLife application uses TikTok's official Content Posting API to publish video content to authorized TikTok accounts automatically.

  • SupremLife only accesses accounts that have explicitly authorized the app.
  • We do not access, read, or store any data belonging to other TikTok users.
  • We do not use TikTok API access to collect follower data, comments, or messages.
  • OAuth tokens used for TikTok authentication are stored securely and used solely for content posting.
  • SupremLife complies fully with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Data Privacy policies.

TikTok API scope: The SupremLife application requests only the minimum permissions required to post video content — specifically the video.publish scope. We do not request access to user data, follower lists, messages, or analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

SupremLife uses the following third-party services to operate our content pipeline:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI-assisted script and caption generation.
  • Google Cloud Text-to-Speech — AI voiceover generation.
  • Pexels — Licensed stock footage for video production.
  • TikTok — Content publishing via the official Content Posting API.
  • Google Sheets — Content logging and scheduling.

5. Data Security

We take the security of any operational data seriously. API keys, access tokens, and credentials used in the SupremLife automation pipeline are stored in secure, encrypted environments, never exposed publicly, and rotated regularly.

6. Children's Privacy

SupremLife content is intended for audiences aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from individuals under the age of 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revisions.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions about SupremLife, contact us through our official TikTok channel @supremelife8.
