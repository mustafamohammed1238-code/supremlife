Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The short version: SupremLife does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. We publish luxury lifestyle content on TikTok using automated tools. We do not access data from TikTok users beyond what is strictly necessary to post to our own account.

This Privacy Policy explains how SupremLife ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our content platform and TikTok integration. By using or interacting with our services, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

SupremLife is a luxury lifestyle content platform that creates and publishes daily video content across social media platforms, primarily TikTok. Our content focuses on supercars, luxury timepieces, private aviation, and high-end travel — targeting audiences aged 18–30.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

SupremLife is a content publishing platform, not a data collection service. We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information from viewers.
  • Store payment or financial information of any kind.
  • Access, collect, or store data from other TikTok users' accounts or profiles.
  • Track individual users across websites or platforms.
  • Build audience profiles or sell data to third parties.
  • Use cookies for tracking purposes on this website.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

SupremLife uses TikTok's official Content Posting API to publish video content to our own TikTok account automatically. In connection with this integration:

  • We only access our own TikTok account credentials and posting permissions.
  • We do not access, read, or store any data belonging to other TikTok users.
  • We do not use TikTok API access to collect follower data, comments, or messages.
  • OAuth tokens used for TikTok authentication are stored securely and used solely for content posting.
  • We comply fully with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Data Privacy policies.

TikTok API scope: Our application requests only the minimum permissions required to post video content — specifically the video.publish scope. We do not request access to user data, follower lists, messages, or analytics.

4. Website Analytics

This website may use basic anonymised analytics to understand visitor traffic (such as page views and general geographic regions). This data is:

  • Fully anonymised — no individual user is identifiable.
  • Used only to improve our website and content strategy.
  • Never shared with or sold to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

SupremLife uses the following third-party services to operate our content pipeline. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI-assisted script and caption generation.
  • ElevenLabs — AI voiceover generation from text scripts.
  • Creatomate — Automated video assembly and rendering.
  • TikTok — Content publishing via the official Content Posting API.
  • Google (Sheets & Drive) — Content logging and file storage.

None of these services receive personal data of our viewers through our integration. They are used solely for content creation and publishing purposes.

6. Data Security

We take the security of any operational data seriously. API keys, access tokens, and credentials used in our automation pipeline are:

  • Stored in secure, encrypted environments.
  • Never exposed publicly or shared with unauthorised parties.
  • Rotated regularly as a security best practice.

7. Children's Privacy

SupremLife content is intended for audiences aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has interacted with our services in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is typically no personal information to access, correct, or delete. However, if you have any privacy-related concerns about how SupremLife operates, you have the right to:

  • Request clarification on what data, if any, relates to you.
  • Request deletion of any data that may have been inadvertently collected.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our operations or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revisions. Continued use of our platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our official TikTok channel or social media platforms under the name @SupremLife.
